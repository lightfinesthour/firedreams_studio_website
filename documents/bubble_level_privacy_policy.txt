PRIVACY POLICY FOR BUBBLE LEVEL
================================

App:         Bubble Level (studio.firedreams.level)
Developer:   Firedreams Studio
Contact:     dream@firedreams.studio
Effective:   13 July 2026
Last update: 13 July 2026


SUMMARY
-------
Bubble Level collects no data. None. The app has no internet access,
no ads, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services.


DETAILS
-------

1. Data collection
   Bubble Level does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data
   or any other data. The app does not request the internet permission,
   so it is technically unable to send anything off your device.

2. Sensors
   The app reads your device's motion sensors (gravity sensor or
   accelerometer) to measure tilt. These readings are processed in
   real time on your device only and are never stored or shared.

3. Settings
   Your preferences (such as calibration, sound and vibration settings)
   are stored locally on your device only. They are deleted when you
   uninstall the app.

4. Third parties
   The app contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party
   code that collects data. Your purchase of the app is handled entirely
   by Google Play; see Google's privacy policy for how Google processes
   payments.

5. Children
   The app is safe for all ages. Since no data is collected from anyone,
   no data is collected from children.

6. Changes
   If this policy ever changes, the new version will be published at
   this same address with an updated date.

7. Contact
   Questions? Email dream@firedreams.studio.
