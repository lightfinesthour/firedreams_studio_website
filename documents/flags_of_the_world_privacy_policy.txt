PRIVACY POLICY FOR FLAGS OF THE WORLD
=====================================

App:         Flags of the World (com.firedreams.flagsoftheworld)
Developer:   Firedreams Studio
Contact:     dream@firedreams.studio
Effective:   23 July 2026
Last update: 23 July 2026


SUMMARY
-------
Flags of the World collects no data. None. The app has no internet
access, no ads, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services.


DETAILS
-------

1. Data collection
   Flags of the World does not collect, store, or transmit any personal
   data or any other data. The app does not request the internet
   permission, so it is technically unable to send anything off your
   device.

2. Game content
   All 224 flag images are bundled inside the app itself. Nothing is
   downloaded, and no request ever leaves your device while you play.

3. Scores and settings
   Your high scores, the name you optionally type next to a high score,
   and your preferences (such as sound and music toggles) are stored
   locally on your device only. They are never sent anywhere, and they
   are deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Third parties
   The app contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party
   code that collects data. Distribution of the app is handled entirely
   by Google Play; see Google's privacy policy for how Google processes
   downloads.

5. Children
   The app is safe for all ages. Since no data is collected from anyone,
   no data is collected from children.

6. Changes
   If this policy ever changes, the new version will be published at
   this same address with an updated date.

7. Contact
   Questions? Email dream@firedreams.studio.
